Our Story

Founded in 2015, Vitamin Accessories began as a small boutique in Nairobi with a vision to create jewelry that tells a story. Each piece is meticulously crafted by our skilled artisans, combining traditional techniques with contemporary design.

Our name "Vitamin" reflects our belief that beautiful accessories are essential to personal expression - just like vitamins are essential to health.

We source only the finest materials, from ethically-mined gemstones to recycled precious metals, ensuring our creations are as responsible as they are beautiful.
